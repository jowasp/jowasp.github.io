How can you participate as student @OWASP Research?

If you are a student looking for a specific subject to work on please contact sign in to our mailing group @ https://groups.google.com/a/owasp.org/forum/#!forum/owasp-research. You can check the actual research topics or start a new one. We will evaluate the topic and feedback with you. One of our main objectives is to match the actual needs of web security with innovative projects and solutions. Also, through this exercise, the inititaive willl support those selected projects for funding through Grant funds and sponsorship. Check the actual research topics at http://jowasp.github.io/researchtopics.html
